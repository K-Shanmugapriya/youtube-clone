Contact Us

common questions about creating an account, watching and uploading videos, and maintaining your
channel.

Community Help Forum. Experiencing a bug? Take a lookat our Current Site Issues page to see a
list of known issues that we're working to fix.

Grievance Mechanism for users in India


If you want to file a Legal complaint against content hosted on YouTube under applicable local

You may use the Grievance Mechanism to file complaints on a range of issues such as (but not
