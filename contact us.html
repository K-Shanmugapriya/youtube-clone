Grievance Mechanism for users in India


If you want to file a Legal complaint against content hosted on YouTube under applicable local
laws, please visit the dedicated Grievance Mechanism page.


You may use the Grievance Mechanism to file complaints on a range of issues such as (but not
limited to) defamation, copyright, counterfeit, impersonation etc.
